Our Services

Income Tax Services

With over 20 years of experience preparing Income Tax Returns, our office can prepare your personal or business income tax. We have electronic filing and direct deposit services available for fast refunds. Our office is open year-round, and our team is always available to help with any necessary follow up on tax related issues.

Our income tax preparation services do not require an up-front payment. Payment can be made with your refund.

Accounting Services

Accounting services are available for all types of businesses, large or small.

Payroll and bookkeeping services are available for your business. We offer profit and loss statements, balance sheet, sale tax declarations, and accounting letters.

Our expertly trained team is here to help build a clear understanding of your income and other financial statements so that you can focus on running and successful business!

Corporation and LLC Formation

We offer business formation services; our dedicated team will walk you through the process of how to get your business starts. We will take the lead on any necessary state and federal registration forms for this process, including licensing, permits, and MVC corporation codes.

We also offer business dissolution services.

Mortgage Consulting

Our mortgage loan office can help you find the best mortgage solution or refinancing options available to you. With our experience and dedication, you can make your home-ownership dream become a reality!.

Representation before the IRS

Are you being contacted by the IRS? Did you receive a notice that you are being audited? Is the IRS holding your refund without stating a clear reason? As an enrolled agent with years of experience, our office will represent you and your rights in any conversation or procedure with the IRS.

Offer of Compromise

Do you owe more than $10,000 to the IRS? Our agents will work on your behalf to negotiate terms with the IRA and DOA with the goal of settling for less than the full amount. We have won cases for many of our previous clients to pay less money than the full amount owed. Let us help you settle these concerns!.

Individual Taxpayer Number - ITIN

If you are do not yet have a social security number or are awaiting a solution to your immigration case, you can stop by our office and we will help you get you ITIN - Individual Taxpayer Identification Number. Our team will help you complete the application to request or renew your ITIN and answer any question you may have about it.
